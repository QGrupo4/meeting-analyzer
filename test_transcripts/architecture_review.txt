Meeting Date: 2026-06-22

Architecture Review

## Agenda

Discuss microservices vs monolith approach for the new payment system.

## Discussion

The team reviewed two approaches. Microservices offer better scalability but add operational complexity.
Monolith would be faster to ship but harder to scale later.

## Decision

We decided to go with a modular monolith. This gives us a single deployable unit with clear module boundaries.
We can extract services later if needed.
